SCIFURNITURE.INC



Overview


SciFurniture are pioneers in the field of manufacturing of Plywood and Venner products. We started with a simple vision of making a million homes beautiful. We’re here to help you create spaces that mirror who you are. We provide quality furniture at affordable prices. SciFurniture outlets are based in New York, San Francisco, and Salt Lake City.


Mission


The organisation is dedicated to continous research and development of new designs to create products for your comfort. It is a one-stop shop for all furniture related needs of the homes. Customer Obsession, Honesty & Transparency, Action Orientation and Efficiency are our core values.


Products


Our wide range of products include:

  • Sofas and Lounges
  • Dining Tables and Chairs
  • Beds and side tables
  • Wardrobes and shelves
