About Us


Cyrotri Corp began as a small single-branch based Electronics Shop in Delhi on August 5, 2000. Due to constant efforts and good customer care, Cyrotri quickly emerged as a leading Electronics store in entire Delhi within 5 years. Now, we're spread over 7000 cities in India. This website is designed to serve our customers at the comfort of their homes. Buying a product from this site makes absolutely zero difference from buying one in our offline stores, Customers get the same service and trust from us either in offline or online. Every product in our stores and in this site are in perfect working condition but still if any issues occur during the warranty period or in the time of transit, we promise to resolve the issue within just 1-3 days of time. We, at Cyrotri, believe this website gains support from our customers as much as our offline stores did.
